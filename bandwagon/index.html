About

Bandwagon is a powerful tool to manage your Bandwagon host service. It's clean and easy to use. You can control your service any where through your iPhone.

Functions

  • Add/remove VPS.
  • Starts the VPS.
  • Stops the VPS.
  • Reboots the VPS.
  • Kill the VPS.
  • Reinstall the Operating System.
  • Generates and sets a new root password.
  • Sets new hostname.
  • run shell.
  • Create/Delete/Restore/Import snapshot.
  • Assigns a new IPv6 address.
  • Releases specified IPv6 address.
  • Migrate VPS to new location.
  • Retrieve information related to service suspensions.
  • Clear abuse issue identified by record_id and unsuspend the VPS.
